# Biogas

: 6

> **Biogas** is a renewable gas produced by the **anaerobic digestion** of organic matter—such as animal manure, food waste, or plant residues—composed mainly of **methane (CH₄)** and **carbon dioxide (CO₂)**, and used as a fuel for heating, electricity, or upgraded to **biomethane** for transport and storage.
