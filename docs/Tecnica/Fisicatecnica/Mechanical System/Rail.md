Here are some well-known **rail transport manufacturers**:

1. **Siemens Mobility:** Siemens is a global technology company that produces rail vehicles, locomotives, and related infrastructure.
2. **Alstom:** A French multinational company that designs and manufactures a wide range of rail solutions, including high-speed trains, tramways, and signaling equipment.
3. **Bombardier Transportation:** A global transportation company that produces a variety of rail products, including trains, rail vehicles, and rail control solutions.
4. **CRRC Corporation Limited:** CRRC is a Chinese state-owned enterprise and the world's largest supplier of rail transit equipment. It manufactures a wide range of products, including high-speed trains and commuter railcars.
5. **Stadler Rail:** A Swiss manufacturer that specializes in the production of railway rolling stock, including trains, trams, and locomotives.
6. **Hitachi Rail:** A division of the Japanese conglomerate Hitachi, Hitachi Rail manufactures high-speed trains, commuter trains, and signaling systems.
7. **CAF (Construcciones y Auxiliar de Ferrocarriles):** A Spanish company that designs and manufactures railway vehicles, including high-speed trains, commuter trains, and trams.
8. Talgo**:** A Spanish manufacturer known for its high-speed trains and innovative railway technology.
9. **Kawasaki Heavy Industries:** A Japanese company that produces a variety of products, including rolling stock such as trains and locomotives.
10. **Ansaldo STS:** An Italian company specializing in railway signaling and transportation systems.
11. **Hyundai Rotem:** A South Korean company that manufactures rolling stock, including high-speed trains and commuter trains.

Remember to check the most recent sources for the latest information on rail transport manufacturers, as the industry is subject to changes and developments over time.
