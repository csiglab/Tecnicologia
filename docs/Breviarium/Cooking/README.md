# Cooking

> This section explores cooking methods, technologies, and tools that enable food preparation efficiently and safely.

## Cooking Technology

| Task                  | Technical Tool                   | Description                                                                    |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Frying                | Air Fryer                        | Uses hot air circulation to cook food with minimal oil.                        |
| Deep Frying           | Deep Fryer                       | Submerges food in hot oil for fast cooking and crisp texture.                  |
| Baking                | Oven                             | Provides controlled dry heat for cooking or baking a variety of dishes.        |
| Broiling              | Broiler (Oven)                   | Uses direct radiant heat from above to cook food quickly.                      |
| Boiling/Simmering     | Stove, Induction Cooktop         | Heats water or liquids to cook food through immersion.                         |
| Steaming              | Steamer Basket, Electric Steamer | Cooks food with steam, preserving nutrients and moisture.                      |
| Roasting              | Convection Oven                  | Uses circulating hot air to cook food evenly and enhance browning.             |
| Grilling              | Electric Grill, Gas Grill        | Uses direct heat to cook food, often imparting a charred flavor.               |
| Braising              | Dutch Oven, Slow Cooker          | Combines searing and slow cooking in liquid to tenderize meats and vegetables. |
| Pressure Cooking      | Pressure Cooker, Instant Pot     | Uses steam pressure to cook food quickly while preserving nutrients.           |
| Sous-vide Cooking     | Immersion Circulator             | Cooks food in a vacuum-sealed bag in a water bath at precise temperatures.     |
| Sautéing              | Frying Pan, Skillet              | Cooks food quickly with a small amount of fat over high heat.                  |
| Poaching              | Saucepan, Double Boiler          | Gently cooks food in liquid below boiling point, preserving texture.           |
| Blending/Grinding     | Blender, Food Processor          | Mechanically breaks down ingredients into a uniform texture.                   |
| Mixing                | Stand Mixer, Hand Mixer          | Combines ingredients uniformly, often with adjustable speeds and attachments.  |
| Whipping/Emulsifying  | Whisk, Stand Mixer               | Incorporates air into ingredients to achieve light, fluffy textures.           |
| Toasting              | Toaster, Oven                    | Uses dry heat to brown or crisp bread and similar foods.                       |
| Fermenting            | Fermentation Crock, Yogurt Maker | Uses controlled microbial activity to transform ingredients (yogurt, kimchi).  |
| Smoking               | Smoke Oven, Smoker Box           | Cooks food with smoke to add flavor and preserve it.                           |
| Candying/Caramelizing | Saucepan, Candy Thermometer      | Uses heat and sugar to create candies or caramelized foods.                    |
| Drying/Dehydrating    | Food Dehydrator                  | Removes moisture from food to preserve it and concentrate flavors.             |
| Stir-Frying           | Wok, Skillet                     | Quickly cooks small pieces of food with high heat and constant stirring.       |
|Cooking Rice|Rice Cooker|Automates cooking rice by controlling heat and timing, often with steaming functions for other grains and foods.|
