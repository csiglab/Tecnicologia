## Cooking Technique Space

| Technique        | Description                         | Efficiency | Notes                                   |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------- | ---------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Boiling          | Water-based heat transfer           | High       | Works well with grains, legumes, roots. |
| Steaming         | Moist heat with minimal water       | High       | Retains nutrients better.               |
| Pressure cooking | High-temp steam under pressure      | Very High  | Reduces cooking time, saves energy.     |
| Stir-frying      | Quick cooking with small oil amount | Moderate   | Useful for mixed-ingredient meals.      |
| Baking           | Dry heat, long process              | Low        | Not ideal for low-cost systems.         |

## Cooking Technical Object Space

| Category        | Technical Object     | Description                                                                            | Use                                                                             |
| --------------- | -------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Heat Source     | Gas stove            | Common in low-cost households                                                          | Fast, controllable heat                                                         |
| Heat Source     | Electric hotplate    | Moderate cost, lower efficiency                                                        | Suitable for small meals                                                        |
| Heat Source     | Induction cooktop    | High efficiency, low heat loss                                                         | Requires compatible cookware                                                    |
| Pressure Device | Pressure cooker      | Reduces fuel/time by up to 70%                                                         | Essential for legumes and grains                                                |
| Electric Cooker | **Rice cooker**      | Thermostatically controlled vessel that automates water absorption and steaming cycles | Highly efficient for grains and multipurpose cooking (rice, beans, eggs, soups) |
| Container       | Cast iron pot        | Retains heat, durable                                                                  | For long-term use                                                               |
| Container       | Stainless pot        | Corrosion-resistant                                                                    | General purpose                                                                 |
| Auxiliary       | Thermal bag / haybox | Passive heat retention                                                                 | Extends cooking without extra fuel                                              |
| Energy          | Solar cooker         | No fuel cost                                                                           | Dependent on climate                                                            |
| Storage         | Refrigerator         | Preserves perishables                                                                  | Increases system efficiency if available                                        |
