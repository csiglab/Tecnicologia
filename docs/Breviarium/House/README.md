# House

> A **house** is a constructed **technical system** designed to provide controlled environmental conditions for human habitation.

## Structure

> Which are the principal technical elements and systems that compose a house?

| **Technical Element**      | **Description**                                                | **Role**                                                                          |
| -------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Foundation System**      | Structural base anchored to soil (e.g., concrete slab, piles). | Distributes loads and ensures stability.                                          |
| **Structural Frame**       | Load-bearing skeleton (wood, steel, reinforced concrete).      | Supports walls, roof, and floors.                                                 |
| **Building Envelope**      | Exterior skin including walls, windows, doors, and roof.       | Controls exchange of heat, air, and moisture; provides insulation and protection. |
| **Interior Partitions**    | Internal walls and dividers.                                   | Organize spatial zones and privacy.                                               |
| **Floor System**           | Layers of subfloor, insulation, and finish material.           | Provides walking surface and load distribution.                                   |
| **Roof System**            | Trusses, insulation, and covering.                             | Shields the house from weather and manages thermal regulation.                    |
| **HVAC System**            | Heating, ventilation, and air conditioning components.         | Maintains indoor temperature, air quality, and comfort.                           |
| **Plumbing System**        | Network of pipes, fixtures, and drains.                        | Delivers potable water and removes wastewater.                                    |
| **Electrical System**      | Wiring, circuits, lighting, outlets, and panels.               | Distributes power to devices and systems.                                         |
| **Communication Network**  | Cables, routers, and wireless systems.                         | Enables data transfer, security, and automation.                                  |
| **Appliances & Equipment** | Tools for cooking, cleaning, and daily living.                 | Support specific domestic tasks.                                                  |
| **Energy System**          | Connection to grid, solar panels, or storage batteries.        | Supplies and regulates energy flow.                                               |
| **Safety Systems**         | Fire alarms, detectors, locks, and cameras.                    | Protects occupants and assets.                                                    |
| **Finishing Elements**     | Paint, flooring, fixtures, and furniture.                      | Provide aesthetic and ergonomic quality.                                          |
